## Tomato sandwich

It's cheap, it's filling, it's easy. 

### ingredients

- bread
- tomatoes
- salt
- mayonaise
- spices as you see fit

### instructions

Toast the bread.

Slice the tomato. Typically one per sandwich.

Once bread is toasted, spread mayo on bread. 

Put tomatoes on bread. then salt tomatoes.
